FINANCING AVAILABLE

Axel Mechanical Services recognizes home repairs or remodeling projects can be very costly. Oftentimes, HVAC and plumbing systems fail without warning, when you can least afford repairs or replacement. At AMS, we never want our customers to have to choose between having heat or paying for necessities like medications or groceries.

To ease the burden, Axel Mechanical Services has partnered with GreenSky Financial and FTL, offering various financing options that will fit your budget. These plans range from 6 months, 0% interest, if paid in full by the end of the 6 month term, to fixed rate financing for up to 10 years, and several other options in between.

We are here to guide you through the process. After a short online application that involves a soft credit pull, you will know within minutes if your application is likely to be approved before moving on to a hard credit check. Once approved, you can choose what financing plan makes sense for your needs.
